Title: Printable Design for Apparel Products

Overview:
This catalog contains printable apparel items available for custom orders.

Products:
- Adult T-Shirts: Sizes S, M, L, XL, XXL. Colors include white, black, navy, light steel, deep royal, deep red, smoke gray, and ash.
- Kids T-Shirts: Youth sizes S, M, L. Colors include white, black, navy, gray, royal blue, red, and charcoal.
- Toddler T-Shirts: Sizes 2, 3, 4. Colors include white, black, navy, gray, light blue, and pink.
- Infant Onesies: Sizes newborn, 6 months, 12 months, and 18 months. Colors include white, black, navy, gray, light blue, and pink.
- Sweatshirts: Sizes S, M, L, XL, XXL. Colors include black, navy, heather charcoal, heather gray, and gray.

Regional Availability:
- Available regions: AU, CA, EU, NZ, US
- Royal Blue, Red, and Charcoal Kids T-Shirts are only available in CA and US.
- Charcoal Toddler T-Shirts are only available in CA and US.
- Heather Charcoal Sweatshirts are only available in AU, CA, NZ, and US.
- Heather Gray Sweatshirts are only available in AU and NZ.
- Gray Sweatshirts are only available in EU.

Order Rules:
- Minimum order quantity: 1 piece
- Maximum order quantity: 50 pieces

Printing Method:
- Direct-to-garment printing
